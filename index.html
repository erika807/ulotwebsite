About Us

Ulot Home Fragrances is an online candle business based in Juba, South Sudan.
We take orders via whatsapp and do deliveries all over Juba.
We specialize in high-quality customizable candles, offering you a range of scents to choose from. Whether you love sweet, floral, fruity, or refreshing scents — we can customize your candle fragrance to suit your mood, season, or personal preference. Every candle is hand-poured with love, creating a special vibe in every home.
